#define AS7331_SENS_UVA   385.0f

Typical UVA responsivity counts/(µW/cm²) at GAIN=2048x, TIME=64ms.

Base irradiance responsivity values from AS7331 datasheet (DS001047 v4-00) Table in Section 5 "Electrical Characteristics", parameter ReGAIN2048. Units: counts per (µW/cm²) at GAIN=2048x, TIME=64ms (65536 clocks), fCLK=1.024MHz

These are typical values at the peak wavelengths: UVA (λ=360nm): 385 counts/(µW/cm²) UVB (λ=300nm): 347 counts/(µW/cm²) UVC (λ=260nm): 794 counts/(µW/cm²)
